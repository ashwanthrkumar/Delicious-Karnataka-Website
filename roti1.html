Jolad Rotti: North Karnataka

Jolada rotti also known as jawar chi bhakri is a very famous recipe in North Karnataka. It is flatbread prepared from jowar. The traditional way of making this is by patting with hands to get a perfect taste and texture. Jolada rotti is also a staple food in most houses in North Karnataka. It is very light, nutritious and easy to make and is served hot along with butter, salad and chatnipudi.
