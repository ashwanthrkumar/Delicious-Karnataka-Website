Korri Gassi: Mangalore

Korri Gassi is also known as Mangalorean chicken curry and is a signature dish belonging to the bunt community. The word Kori means Chicken and Gassi means curry. To prepare this dish, tender pieces of chicken is combined with mild sweet ground coconut and then cooked with different spices which forms the flavoursome taste of this dish. Korri Gassi is often served with a dry chicken dish on the side called Kori ajadina and is then eaten with either rice, rice wafers (roti) or dosa.
